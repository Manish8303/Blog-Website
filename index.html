About the Project

Businesses today face massive volumes of operational data — from sales figures to employee performance records. Traditional reporting systems are slow and error-prone. BizSync Analytics solves this by providing a unified, intelligent dashboard capable of real-time analysis and visualization. By synchronizing data from multiple sources, the system offers a holistic overview of business performance and empowers management with instant insights.

How It Works

BizSync Analytics connects securely to a MySQL database, extracts data through Express APIs, and visualizes it through an interactive dashboard. Users can log in, monitor operations, and analyze performance in seconds.
